### Definitions and Mappings Guide

#### Suitedness Mapping
- **Rainbow**: 0 (No card of same suit)
- **Two-tone**: 1 (2 of 3 of same suit)
- **Monotone**: 2 (All same suit)

#### Pairness Mapping
- **High Pair**: 3 (AAx-JJx)
- **Middle Pair**: 2 (TTx-77x)
- **Low Pair**: 1 (66x-22x)
- **Unpaired**: 0 (No pair)

#### Connectedness Mapping
- **Three Connected**: 2 (all 3 cards connected)
- **Two Connected**: 1 (2 cards connected)
- **Disconnected**: 0 (no cards connected)
